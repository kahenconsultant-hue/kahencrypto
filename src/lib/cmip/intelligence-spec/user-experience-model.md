# User Experience Model

The report must prioritize decision clarity over length.

## First 30 Seconds

Show:

- today's posture
- plain-language action
- largest positive driver
- largest negative driver
- confidence
- what to watch today

## First 2 Minutes

Show:

- why
- why not
- what changed
- invalidation triggers

## Up To 5 Minutes

Show:

- historical evidence
- scenario matrix
- ten-coin posture
- decision memory
- data-quality audit

Rules:

- The user should see the decision before the machinery.
- Contradiction and uncertainty remain visible.
- The report must not become a long data dump.
- Advanced details are available after the first decision layer.
